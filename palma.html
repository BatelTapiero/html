Weather in Palma de Mallorca
In Palma, the summers are short, hot, muggy, dry, and mostly clear and the winters are long, cold, windy, and partly cloudy. Over the course of the year, the temperature typically varies from 41°F to 87°F and is rarely below 34°F or above 93°F.
Based on the beach/pool score, the best time of year to visit Palma for hot-weather activities is from late June to late August.
